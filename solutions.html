FAQ

Common questions.

What's the typical timeline for an AI implementation?

Most projects: Assess (2-4 weeks), Design (3-6 weeks), Build (6-12 weeks), Deploy (2-4 weeks). Exact timeline depends on complexity, data readiness, and team capacity.

Do you work with specific AI platforms or tools?

We remain platform-agnostic. Recommendations are based on your requirements—OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source, or proprietary. We evaluate on fit, cost, and risk.

What if we don't have clean data?

Data quality issues are common. We audit during assessment and build cleaning into implementation. Bad data is a known problem we solve for.

Can you help if we're just starting with AI?

Absolutely. We start with assessment, help you understand what's possible, and build internal capacity. You won't be dependent on us forever.

How do you handle change management?

Technical implementation is only part of the challenge. We document processes, deliver training, and ensure your team can use and maintain everything we build.
