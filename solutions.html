FAQ

Common questions.

How long does an AI Readiness Assessment take?

Typically 2–4 weeks. We conduct stakeholder interviews, audit your data and infrastructure, and deliver a written report with scored dimensions and a prioritized recommendation roadmap.

Do you work with mid-market companies or only enterprise?

Both. Our assessment and strategy engagements are well-suited for mid-market companies ($20M–$500M revenue), while implementation and managed operations work best for enterprise scale. We’ll be honest if we’re not the right fit.

What industries do you specialize in?

Financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and technology are our strongest verticals. We have deep domain expertise in regulated industries where data governance and compliance are non-negotiable.

Can you work with our existing technology team?

Yes, and we prefer it. The most successful engagements embed us alongside your team. We transfer knowledge as we build, so you own and understand every system we deliver.

What does a typical implementation timeline look like?

Most mid-scale AI implementations run 3–6 months from design to production. We use phased delivery so you see working software every 2–3 weeks, not just at the end.

Begin with a no-cost conversation. We’ll assess your situation honestly and tell you exactly what we can and can’t do for you.
