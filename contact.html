Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Proxiant

How quickly can you start a project?

Most engagements begin within 2-3 weeks of signing. Our streamlined onboarding process ensures we can quickly understand your requirements, assemble the right team, and hit the ground running. For urgent projects, we offer expedited timelines with dedicated resources.

Do you work with startups or only enterprises?

We work with organizations of all sizes—from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our flexible engagement models and scalable team structure allow us to deliver value whether you need a focused project team or comprehensive transformation support. We're committed to helping you succeed regardless of your stage.

What's your typical engagement length?

Projects range from 4-week assessments to 12+ month implementations. We tailor engagement length to your needs, whether you're seeking a strategic assessment, proof of concept, or a full production rollout. Many clients continue with our Managed Operations service for ongoing optimization and support.

Can you work with our existing tech stack?

Absolutely. We're technology-agnostic and integrate seamlessly with your existing infrastructure. Whether you're using cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP, legacy systems, or custom architectures, our engineers have deep expertise across all major stacks. We prioritize solutions that fit your environment.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, our Managed Operations service provides continuous support, monitoring, and optimization after deployment. We offer flexible SLAs and can scale support based on your needs. Many clients value the peace of mind of having experienced engineers managing their systems and continuously improving performance.
