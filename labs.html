ADAPTIVE TRADING

ProxiTrades

Adaptive Multi-Strategy Trading System

TradingView-triggered, broker-routed (Alpaca, IBKR), with a five-loop strategy generator that mints, validates, and retires strategies faster than the market arbitrages them away. Every day at 20:00 UTC the system writes a structured report — what worked, what didn't, why — with LLM-suggested config diffs you can review and execute.

  • V7 baseline: OOS Sharpe 2.06, 30.2% annualized, 6% max DD
  • Walk-forward validated on real Alpaca data, 4-fold OOS
  • Adversarial Bull/Bear/Referee debate on every hypothesis
  • Daily learning report — emailed, persisted, with applied-diff trail
  • Three-layer kill switch (operator CLI, dead-man, broker bracket)
