Our Story

Building the future of enterprise AI — one partnership at a time.

"We believe AI should work for everyone — not just those with PhD teams and billion-dollar budgets."

Proxiant was founded on a simple belief: enterprise AI shouldn't be the exclusive domain of tech giants and well-funded startups. We saw brilliant organizations with real business problems, but lacked the expertise and partnerships to navigate the AI landscape.

Our mission is to democratize access to world-class AI expertise and production-grade AI products. We partner with enterprises to move from AI curiosity to AI competency — delivering measurable business outcomes at scale.

We envision a future where every organization, regardless of size or industry, can harness AI as a sustainable competitive advantage. That's why we invest in deep partnerships, build tools that stick, and mentor teams to thrive in an AI-first world.

Our Journey

From a consulting practice to a global AI powerhouse — here's how we got here.

2022

Founded

Started as an AI consulting practice with a mission to help enterprises move beyond pilots and proofs of concept.

2023

First Enterprise Client

Delivered comprehensive AI strategy transformation for a Fortune 500 financial services organization, setting the blueprint for our enterprise practice.

2023

Proxiant Labs Launched

Released ProxiFlow and ProxiAgent, our flagship AI platforms designed to accelerate enterprise deployment and reduce time-to-value.

2024

ProxiAlpha Launched

Introduced our AI-powered trading platform featuring 15+ proprietary strategies, bringing AI to the financial markets with measurable returns.

2025

100+ Projects

Crossed the milestone of 100 enterprise AI implementations, serving customers across finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and beyond.

2026

Global Expansion

Opened offices in Austin (HQ), New York, and London to better serve our global enterprise customer base and strengthen our engineering teams.
